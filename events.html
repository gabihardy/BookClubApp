Your turn to host?

Find and click your name in the "Scheduled Events" to enter your book choice

We have built in a "courtesy" time window to allow each member ample time to acquire and read monthly book titles. As a host, you are able to change your book choice up to 3 months in advance of the meeting date. At that point, your book choice is locked in and members are encouraged to acquire and read the book.
